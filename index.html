Hi, my name is Alex web developer & designer

who i am

a strategic visual story teller

Ever since I can remember, I've been looking for each and every opportunity to help people tell their stories. Stories bring us together, grow and challenge our perspectives, and overall make us and our world better. In 2019, my preferred medium for story telling is code. Why code? Well, code is versatile and powerful--just like people. I know that when the two are paired together, they can build bridges, cities, and worlds.

an adventure seeker

I am a fiend for new experiences. Whether it be visiting a new country, learning a new coding language, or making a new friend, newness is my lifeblood. Nothing brings me more joy than learning something new and getting to share it with others.

What I do

01 | get inspired

I help people hash out ideas and come up with creative solutions to real world problems.

02 | strategize

I take those ideas to the white board and spend time architecting, designing, strategizing, and ideating.

03 | develop

Using my full stack of languages, I build apps and websites from the ground up.

04 | rinse & repeat

Do what you love, and love what you do. I love design, I love code, and I love people. Let's keep this going.
